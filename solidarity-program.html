Solidarity Program

Participate in our "Offer a Sheep" program to help families in need.

What is the Solidarity Program?

The "Offer a Sheep" Solidarity Program is an initiative that allows any citizen to make a donation to offer a sheep to a family in need during holiday periods.

You can choose to fully fund a sheep or contribute partially. Every contribution counts and helps bring joy to disadvantaged families.

All beneficiary families are validated by local authorities (APC) or recognized associations to ensure that aid reaches those who truly need it.

Make a Donation

Contribute to offering a sheep to a family in need. You can make a full or partial donation.

Beneficiary Families

Beneficiary families are selected and validated by local authorities and associations.

Transparency

Track the impact of your donation. We keep you informed about the family that benefited from your generosity.

Our Goal for 2025

Sheep Donated 250 / 500

We aim to offer 500 sheep to families in need this year. Thanks to your generosity, we have already reached 50% of our goal.

Make a Donation

Fill out the form below to make a donation and offer a sheep to a family in need.

The average price of a sheep is 35,000 DZD.

Thank you for your donation!

Your generosity will make a difference for a family in need.

We will send you an email with details about the family that benefited from your donation.

A tax receipt will also be sent to you for your tax declaration.

Our Impact

Thanks to the generosity of our donors, we have been able to offer sheep to hundreds of families in need throughout Algeria.

Each donation makes a real difference in the lives of beneficiary families, allowing them to celebrate holidays with dignity and share moments of joy with their loved ones.
