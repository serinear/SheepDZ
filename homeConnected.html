User Guide

How to use the Application

> Follow these simple steps to reserve your sheep easily and securely

1

Reserve Your Spot

Click "Join the list" to begin your reservation process.

2

Complete the Form

Enter your personal details and select priority option if applicable.

3

Get Your appointment

You will receive your appointment details by SMS, and you can always access them on your account.

4

Visit the Market

Come to the location on time, show your ID card , and choose your sheep with no time wasted.

5

Waitlist Option

If the main list is closed, you can join the waitlist. You'll be notified by SMS if a spot becomes available.
